About

Roberta Antinolfi (1999) is a graphic and editorial designer based in Italy, currently attending the MA in Communication and Design for Publishing at ISIA U.

With a BA in Graphic Design at IED Roma, her work focuses on printed matter, books, web developing, data and identities. After graduating, she spent a year working at Studio Folder where she deepened her curiosity in cartography and typography, and added a pragmatic touch to her creative approach.

When she's not working, you might catch her lost in the worlds of video games, cinema, cuisine and table games. Curiosity and contemplation fuel her persona, guiding her towards ever more ambitious goals.
